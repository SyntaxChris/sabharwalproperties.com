Welcome
Sabharwal Properties is a family owned real estate investment and development company where people and their passions unite.
Founded in 1984, we are innovative and versatile real estate operators with rich and diverse experience through every asset class and market cycle.  That experience has proved invaluable to our family, investors, and the communities benefited by our projects.  Please feel free to browse through our site to learn more about us and our projects.
Meet The Team

Harsimran Singh Sabharwal

Harsimran Singh Sabharwal, the founder of our company, is a successful entrepreneur with varied business interests, as well as a noted author. Dr. Sabharwal has been active in the real estate industry for nearly three decades and has extensive experience in all forms of real estate investment, from residential to retail, multifamily, industrial, office and hospitality.

Previous to real estate, he worked as a Design Engineer for several major US firms with a specialty in powerplant design. Dr. Sabharwal received his Ph.D. in Business Economics from Pacific Western University, Pasadena, CA in 1986. His personal passion in life lies in his writings, which focus on such wide ranging topics as the power of prayer, spirituality and positive thinking, to real estate foreclosures and billionaire immigrant success stories. His books can be found at www.drsinghbooks.com.

Gunit S. Sabharwal

Gunit S. Sabharwal is actively involved in all aspects of the company's real estate operations, from finance and management, to acquisition and leasing. His hands-on approach and attention to detail have helped him to serve as a multifaceted director of the company's operations as well as the company's general counsel. In that capacity, Gunit manages the company's entitlements and zoning processes with regards to its developments throughout the country.

Gunit completed his undergraduate degree at Polytechnic University (currently Polytechnic Institute of NYU), graduated from Hofstra University School of Law in 2006 and is currently completing a Master's of Science in Finance degree at Georgetown University. He has been a member of the NYS Bar since 2007. He can be reached at gunit@sabharwalproperties.com.

Pavit S. Sabharwal

Pavit S. Sabharwal is actively involved in all operations of Sabharwal Properties with a major focus being on acquisitions as well as being jointly responsible for leasing and property management of the company's own 1M+ square foot commercial portfolio.

Pavit is a graduate of Babson College with a focus on entrepreneurship, and received a Master's degree in Real Estate from New York University, with a concentration in Finance and Investment. Pavit has brought a unique insight to Sabharwal Properties, focusing primarily on the acquisition on under-valued and under-utilized commercial real estate throughout New York and the Southeastern United States. He can be reached at pavit@sabharwalproperties.com
