Welcome

Sabharwal Properties is a family owned real estate investment and development company where people and their passions unite.

Founded in 1984, we are innovative and versatile real estate operators with rich and perse experience through every asset class and market cycle.  That experience has proved invaluable to our family, investors, and the communities benefited by our projects.  Please feel free to browse through our site to learn more about us and our projects.

Meet The Team

Harsimran Singh Sabharwal, P.E., Ph.D.

President and founder of our company, is a successful entrepreneur and noted author. Dr. Sabharwal has been active in the real estate industry for over 30 years and has extensive experience in nearly all forms of real estate investment and market cycles.

Under his leadership, the company has successfully completed hundreds of projects covering nearly every real estate asset class, including multifamily, retail, industrial and hospitality.

Dr. Sabharwal holds an undergraduate degree in civil engineering from Punjab Engineering College, Chandigarh, India (1968). He also holds a Ph.D. in Business Economics from Pacific Western University, Pasadena, CA (1986). He is a licensed NYS Professional Engineer.

Previous to real estate, he worked as a civil engineer for several major US firms with a specialty in the design and construction of power plants. His personal passion in life lies in his writings, which focus on such wide ranging topics as the power of prayer, spirituality and positive thinking, to real estate foreclosures and billionaire immigrant success stories. His books can be found at www.drsinghbooks.com. He can be reached at drsaab@sabharwalproperties.com.

Punit K. Sabharwal, Esq.

Vice President, maintains joint responsibility for the sourcing and development of new opportunities for the company. Punit utilizes her diverse background in real estate law, design and development to approach new projects from variety of perspectives.

Previous to Sabharwal Properties, Punit practiced law as an associate with Forchelli, Curto, Crowe Deegan in Mineola, NY where she represented transactional clients in a corporate real estate law office, including representation of the Ritz Carlton residences of Long Island.

Subsequently, she joined the international luxury design/build firm of Juneja Associates, where she designed and executed luxury homes, hotels and retail spaces for some of the premier retailers and high-net-worth clients in Asia, including Salvatore Ferragamo, Christian Dior, W Hotels, and The Pierre among others.

Punit holds the following degrees/licenses:

Bachelor of Science, Economics, Hofstra University
Juris Doctor, New York Law School

Licensed Attorney, New York State Bar Association (2006)

Professional Interior Design Diploma, Rhodec International School of Interior Design

She can be reached at punit@sabharwalproperties.com.

Gunit S. Sabharwal, Esq.

Vice President and General Counsel, actively manages the real estate operations of the company, including negotiating joint ventures, purchase and sale agreements, leasing transactions and financing agreements. Gunit manages the contractual processes, zoning and due diligence work involved in undertaking new ventures and underwriting potential acquisitions.

Gunit also maintains primary responsibility over the company’s legal compliance, auditor compliance and financial reporting mechanisms. With a background in software development, he ensures the company’s internal and external financial reporting mechanisms remain on the cutting edge, with the goals of transparency, compliance and intuitiveness.

Gunit holds the following degrees/licenses:

Bachelor of Science, Computer Science, NYU Tandon School of Engineering (formerly Polytechnic University)

Master of Science, Finance, Georgetown University, McDonough School of Business

Juris Doctor, Hofstra University Maurice A. Deane School of Law
Licensed Attorney, New York State Bar Association (2007)

He can be reached at gunit@sabharwalproperties.com.

Pavit S. Sabharwal

Vice President, is actively involved the company’s operations with a distinct focus on acquisitions, construction and asset management. Pavit approaches acquisitions and asset management with a keen eye on the development and construction feasibility of a project. Since 2009, he has overseen over 100 residential and commercial construction projects with a disciplined approach to value engineering.

Pavit holds the following degrees:

Bachelor of Science, Babson College with a focus on Business Administration

Master of Science, Real Estate, New York University Schack Institute of Real Estate

Master of Science, Construction Administration, Columbia University (Expected 2017)

He can be reached at pavit@sabharwalproperties.com.
